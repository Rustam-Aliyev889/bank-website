Choose Your Strategy

Value Investing

  • Long-term opportunity for large gains.
  • Stronger risk/reward relationships.
  • Analysis supported by financial metrics.
  • Dividends aren't reliant on cash for growth.

Growth Investing

  • Shorter-term capital appreciation.
  • The sharpest and greatest stock price increases.
  • Good for beginners.
  • Once growth begins, future periods of continued growth are more likely.

Momentum Investing

  • Higher risk means higher reward.
  • Momentum trading is done in the short-term.
  • It doesn't rely on bigger picture elements.
  • It's often the most exciting style of trading.
